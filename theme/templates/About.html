About Me

Hey everyone! I’m Arkadeep Ganguly, a third-year Computer Science student at MIT Manipal. I built this tool scraper during my internship at Keyline DigiTech Pvt. Ltd. as a hands-on project to automate business data extraction.

The tool is coded in Python, using modules like pyautogui and selenium, and even implements multithreading to speed things up. It’s designed to work specifically with Google Business Profile pages in India, based on the initial project requirements — but it’s scalable if needed in the future.

The only current limitation is bypassing CAPTCHAs, which requires residential proxies for rotating IPs — and those can get expensive. Apart from that, everything works smoothly.

If you’ve reached my site and have questions, feel free to get in touch — I’d love to hear your feedback or help you out. Thanks for checking it out!
